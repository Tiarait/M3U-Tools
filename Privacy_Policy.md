# Privacy Policy — M3U Tools

**Last updated:** June 2026
**Contact:** [tiar.develop@gmail.com](mailto:tiar.develop@gmail.com)

M3U Tools is an Android application for viewing, organizing, and editing IPTV playlists (M3U/M3U8) locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes and how it is used.

## Summary

* No account registration is required.
* The app does not operate its own servers for storing user playlists.
* Playlist data remains on your device unless you explicitly export, share, back up, or transfer it.
* Internet access is used only for features that require network communication.
* Optional rewarded advertisements may be shown through Google AdMob if you choose to watch an ad.
* Optional purchases are processed through Google Play Billing.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally:

| Data                                      | Purpose                                              |
| ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Playlist metadata and channel information | Playlist management and editing                      |
| Imported M3U/M3U8 files                   | Local storage and export                             |
| Application settings                      | Preferences such as theme, language, and behavior    |
| Recently used playlist URLs               | Easier re-importing                                  |
| Cached images and logos                   | Faster display of channel logos and playlist artwork |

Imported playlists may contain information supplied by your IPTV provider, including stream URLs, credentials embedded in URLs, EPG links, and other provider-specific metadata. You are responsible for the content you import into the app.

## Network Usage

The app accesses the internet only when you use features that require network communication.

| Feature                          | Information Sent                                           |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Import from URL                  | Request sent to the playlist URL you provide               |
| Refresh playlist                 | Request sent to the playlist source URL                    |
| URL availability check           | Request sent to selected stream URLs                       |
| Logo preview                     | Request sent to logo URLs referenced by playlists          |
| Optional rewarded advertisements | AdMob SDK may communicate with Google advertising services |
| Optional purchases               | Google Play Billing communicates with Google Play services |

Requests are made directly from your device to the specified servers. We do not operate a service that receives, stores, or processes your playlist data.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

* Google AdMob (optional rewarded advertisements)
* Google Play Billing (optional purchases)
* Google Play Services

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Advertising

The free version of the app may offer optional rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob. Ads are shown only when you choose to watch them.

Google and its advertising partners may collect certain device and advertising identifiers as described in Google's privacy documentation. Where required by law, consent choices are managed through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP).

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

| Permission    | Purpose                                                                |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Internet      | Playlist import, refresh, URL checks, logo loading, ads, and purchases |
| Notifications | Optional notifications for background operations                       |

The app does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, or device location.

## Data Deletion

You can remove data stored by the app at any time by:

* Deleting playlists individually within the app.
* Using the "Reset All Data" option in app settings.
* Clearing app data through Android system settings.
* Uninstalling the application.

Because the app does not maintain user accounts or cloud storage, deleting local app data or uninstalling the app removes information stored by the app on the device.

## Security

We value your trust in using the app and strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect information stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and appropriate steps will be taken to address the issue.
